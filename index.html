TheoryScope
A curated library of 40 canonical IO Psychology and Management theories — JD-R, SDT, RBV, Sensemaking, Psychological Safety, the lot — with four views on the same data: Browse (filter by discipline + level), AI Matchmaker (paste your RQ → ranked fits), Comparator (side-by-side + 10 famous debates), and an Influence Map (force-directed network of builds-on / rival / critiques edges). Tracewise
Map the work you actually do — not the SOP, the practical as-is process. A hybrid interview (structured questions + adaptive AI probes) traces each step, tags its friction, and surfaces shadow tools. Then the Automation-Fit engine scores every step for where AI actually belongs — quick win, big bet, leave it, or protect (human judgment) — so AI is aimed only where it genuinely earns its place. The Ecosystem view stitches multiple role-captures into one map and stress-tests it: if the work had to grow 10–15× in 18 months, what breaks first?
